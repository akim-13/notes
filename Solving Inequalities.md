# Solving Inequalities
**Q:** Solve $\frac{1}{x-1} + \frac{2}{x+1} < 1$
**A:** 

$$
\begin{flalign}
    & \frac{1}{x-1} + \frac{2}{x+1} < 1 \quad \biggr| \times (x-1)^{2}(x+1)^{2} && \\
    & (x-1)(x+1)^{2} + 2(x-1)^{2}(x+1) - (x-1)^{2}(x+1)^{2} < 0 && \\ 
    & (x-1)(x+1)(x^{2}-3x) > 0 && \\
    & x(x-3)(x-1)(x+1) > 0 &&  \\
\end{flalign}
$$
